# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Demo viewers and potential users exploring market trading interfaces. They are looking for a clear way to monitor and interact with high-volatility market data in real-time.

## Product Purpose
A demo market trading dashboard designed to showcase high-frequency data handling. The primary goal is to present complex, volatile market movements in a way that is clear, presentable, and instantly recognizable as a professional-grade trading tool.

## Brand Personality
Clean, approachable, and high-frequency. It should feel responsive and "alive" with data, but remain calm and expert-focused rather than frantic or cluttered.

## Anti-references
- **AI Slop**: Avoid generic, over-stylized, or incoherent AI-generated aesthetics.
- **Tech-First Demos**: It should not look like a "list of technologies" or a basic boilerplate.
- **Visual Overload**: No unnecessary gradients, shadows, or "glassmorphism" that distracts from the data.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: High-frequency data streams must be legible. Use precise typography and deliberate spacing to manage cognitive load.
- **Instant Context**: Users should immediately recognize the app's purpose through established trading UI patterns (charts, order books, watchlists).
- **Approachable Expertise**: Present professional-grade volatility curves in a clean, accessible layout that invites exploration.
- **Product, Not Platform**: Focus entirely on the trading experience and data utility, keeping the underlying tech stack invisible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High-contrast data visualization for readability.
- Clear semantic structure for screen readers in complex data tables.
- Consider reduced motion options for high-frequency chart updates if needed.
